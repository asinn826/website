ABOUT ME


I'm a 19-year old student currently pursuing a Combined Major in Business and Computer Science at the University of British Columbia in Vancouver, Canada. Due to the unique nature of my undergraduate degree, I get exposure to both the business and computer science worlds, allowing me to pair my business acumen accrued from my time in business school with my technical knowledge gained from my computer science courses.

At the Sauder School of Business at UBC, I have been exposed to numerous core business courses including Finance, Accounting, Management Information Systems, and Information Systems Analysis and Design. These courses have helped me build a strong understanding of various aspects of the business world, ultimately helping me develop a strong, universally applicable, business toolkit.


Business skills are not all that I know, however. I have also been exposed to several programming languages such as Java, C++, and Racket, and have had the chance to apply my knowledge of Java in the completion of an Android app for one of my software construction courses. I also have experience working with HTML and CSS to create beautiful and functional web pages.

Outside of school, I have had numerous opportunities to apply the skills I gained in the classroom in various extracurricular activities. I have been involved in various activities ranging from roles within the Commerce Undergraduate Society to team-based Case Competitions, which have provided me with many opportunities to successfully leverage my unique toolkit to stand out among my peers and gain recognition within the professional community.

When I'm not throwing together lines of code on the screen or fiddling with spreadsheets, I can be found tinkering with my PC or enjoying the beautiful outdoors.
